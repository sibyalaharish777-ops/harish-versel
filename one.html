job roles

A job role is a set of duties and responsibilities assigned to an employee in an organization. Different job roles require different skills, qualifications, and experience. For example, a teacher educates students, a doctor provides medical care, a software developer creates applications, and an accountant manages financial records. Every job role contributes to the success of a company or society. Choosing the right job role based on a person's interests and abilities can lead to career growth, job satisfaction, and professional success.
